Nurturing better minds and better ideas, for a better world.



The eiguild is a collaborative working network of investors, entrepreneurs, professionals, trainers, institutions, industry and the students.
We aims towords nurturing entrepreneurship, incubating innovative ideas and providing investors with investment opportunities on these future making ideas. The guild has activities in the areas of identification, assessment, overall development, refinement and certification of talents and skills. It thereby eases the talent aquisition process across industries.
